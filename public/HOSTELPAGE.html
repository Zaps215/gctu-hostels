Yes! Every listing is physically inspected by our team and re-verified annually. We check safety, cleanliness, and amenities so you get exactly what you book.

All payments are processed via secure gateway. You pay the hostel rent plus a small service fee (transparent). We never charge hidden fees.

Absolutely — use our filters to search by university, distance in kilometers, or neighborhood. We cover KNUST, UG, UCC, Ashesi, UPSA, GIMPA and more.
